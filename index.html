Virtual Human Demo

During my time as a Ph.D. candidate, I developed a low-cost reconstruction method for realistic 3D avatars, a statistical model for body-weight modification to investigate the potential of VR therapy in obesity treatment, as well as anatomical representations of virtual humans. Click the Load Demo button for an interactive application that showcases various parts of our virtual human reconstruction pipeline (may take a few seconds).
